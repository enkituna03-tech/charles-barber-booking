# Charles Barber — Online Booking

A simple appointment-booking website. Customers pick a service and time, and the
request is emailed straight to the shop — no email app, no backend.

## Edit your details
All settings live in one `CONFIG` block at the top of **`script.js`**:
business name, contact email, services & prices, and opening hours.

## How requests reach you
Bookings are delivered by [FormSubmit](https://formsubmit.co) (free, no signup).

**One-time setup:** the first booking triggers an activation email from FormSubmit
to the shop inbox — open it and click **"Activate Form"** once. Every booking after
that arrives automatically.

## Run locally
Just open `index.html` in a browser.
